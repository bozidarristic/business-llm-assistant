# Internal Business Policies

## Refund Policy

Refunds are available only for annual subscriptions within the first 30 days from contract signature. Refunds require approval from Finance and Customer Success. Refunds are not normally available for monthly plans unless there is a documented service outage longer than 24 hours.

## SLA Policy

Enterprise customers receive first response within 4 business hours for high-priority tickets. Professional customers receive first response within 8 business hours for high-priority tickets. Starter customers receive best-effort support during business hours.

If an SLA breach occurs, the Customer Success Manager should acknowledge the delay, apologize clearly, explain the next action, and provide a realistic follow-up timeline.

## Data Privacy Policy

Customer data must not be sent to external AI services without written customer approval. For sensitive customer data, local models or approved private deployments should be used. Logs should avoid storing full customer records unless required for debugging and approved by the account owner.

## Lead Prioritization Guide

High-priority leads usually have at least one of the following characteristics: estimated value above 50,000 EUR, strong privacy requirements where local deployment is valuable, active proposal stage, or a clear urgent business problem. Low-priority leads usually have unclear budget, unclear timeline, or weak product fit.

## Customer Health Guide

A customer should be considered at renewal risk if the health score is below 50, if multiple high-priority tickets remain open, or if the latest notes mention dissatisfaction, SLA problems, or unresolved integration issues.
